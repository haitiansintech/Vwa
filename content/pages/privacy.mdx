## What We Collect

When you subscribe to updates, we collect your email address. We use it only to send you information about Haitian civic events and Vwa updates.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except as required by law.

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies for language preference and analytics. No advertising cookies are used.

## Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

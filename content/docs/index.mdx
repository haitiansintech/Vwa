---

Vwa provides clear, sourced civic information to help Haitians understand and participate in Haiti's electoral process.

## What You'll Find Here

- Eligibility guidance for diaspora Haitians
- Documentation requirements and how to obtain them
- An election timeline updated as information becomes available
- Context on Haiti's political institutions and key figures

[Check your eligibility](/eligibility) or [browse resources](/resources) to get started.

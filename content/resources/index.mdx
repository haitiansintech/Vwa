## Overview

This section collects key resources for Haitians who want to understand the electoral process, their rights as citizens, and how to participate.

## Electoral Process

Haiti's electoral system is overseen by the Conseil Electoral Provisoire (CEP). Elections follow a constitutional framework that grants voting rights to all Haitian nationals regardless of where they live.

## Voter Registration

Information on voter registration requirements and timelines will be updated as official guidance becomes available from the CEP.

## Diaspora Participation

Haitians living abroad hold full citizenship rights. The eligibility of diaspora Haitians to vote depends on several factors including citizenship documentation and registration.
